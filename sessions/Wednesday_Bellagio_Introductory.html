TLC201-L - Leadership Session: Telecom - Empowering Transformation at the Cusp of Radical Technology Advancement The Telecom industry is at the cusp of major transformation. On the technological side, the advent of 5G networks with the promise for massive mobile broadband, billions of connected devices, and ultra-low latency applications presents a unique opportunity, but also requires changes to core Telecom systems for operational and business support. On the business side, there are competitive market forces and ecosystem disruptors that challenge the industry to explore new avenues for cost optimization and revenue generation. In this keynote panel, join AWS customers from the telecom industry, along with Jean-Philippe Poirault, an industry veteran who leads an AWS team focused the on the telecom industry, as they discuss their approach to this transformation and how the AWS Cloud enables and accelerates their strategic priorities.
